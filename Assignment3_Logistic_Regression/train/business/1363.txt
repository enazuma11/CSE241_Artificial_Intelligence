A row between Tesco and Unilever over pricing has left shoppers unable to buy a wide range of household staples on the Tesco website. Unilever, one of Britain’s biggest suppliers of branded food and household goods, is trying to charge Tesco 10% more for its products after the pound’s fall against the euro and the dollar following the Brexit vote.
