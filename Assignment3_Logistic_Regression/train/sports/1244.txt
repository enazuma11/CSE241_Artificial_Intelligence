 The British No2, Dan Evans, has lost to German Tobias Kamke in the first round of the Aegon International at Eastbourne Evans, who has a wildcard into Wimbledon, was unable to make inroads on his opponent’s serve and went down 6-2, 6-3. Ivo Karlovic was also a first-round casualty, the giant Croat, seeded five, losing 6-4, 6-4 to France’s Jérémy Chardy. In the women’s event, the fifth seed, Angelique Kerber, survived two close sets to beat Alison Riske 7-6, 6-4.
