Liverpool manager Jürgen Klopp defends Wayne Rooney’s late night socialising and says the current generation of footballers is the most professional the game has ever seen. “All the legends we love and admire drank like devils and smoked like crazy,” says Klopp at a news conference at Melwood on Thursday. England captain Rooney apologised “unreservedly” to the FA earlier in the week for being photographed at a wedding in the early hours of Sunday despite the fact he was granted the time off by manager Gareth Southgate
