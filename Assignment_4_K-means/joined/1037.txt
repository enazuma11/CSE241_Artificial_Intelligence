John Chilcot, the head of the inquiry into Britain’s role in the Iraq War explains why it took far longer than expected, ahead of the report’s official publication on July 6. He says: “The scale of the task proved absolutely massive. We assembled a base of evidence of something like 150,000 government documents”.
