Michael Gove simply says ‘yes’, when asked if he would publish his tax return, while Andrea Leadsom is more hesitant, saying she would but she does not want to set a precedent for all MPs. Speaking on BBC1’s The Andrew Marr Show, Leadsom says she does not believe that people should have to disclose everything in life and MPs are under ‘far too much pressure’. It is expected that the publication of their tax returns will happen before the declaration of the new Conservative Party leader on 9 September
